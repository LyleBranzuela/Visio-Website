About Us

Visio was formed in 1999, Visio specializes in crafting glass products, be it for mirrors or for windows. Visio always made all of their products when thinking about what is their customer’s needs, be it design or for multiple use.

By accepting feedbacks, reviews, and some even personal experiences, Visio always strived for what’s best for the customers, with our slogan as “The Vision Of Your Future” we always look at your current perspective as well as future perspectives before we look at our perspective first.

As long as possible, materials are sourced from companies with reliable and trusted materials, by accepting both local and overseas materials, Visio helps grow local products and to introduce new materials from overseas to local companies to use to improve their own materials, leading to helping the local market grow.

IMIN

The company IMIN joined Visio since 2008, the time where the film “Journey to the Center of Earth” was made 3D, giving the rise to 3D films. IMIN was created since 1999, starting out with a small theatre building in North Wellington. If you want to watch various films from 3D to 2D, IMIN is definitely a good choice for you as IMIN is spread throughout New Zealand.

IMIN contacted Visio and suggested an idea that our company, that was mainly making glass products at the time to invest on glasses, as glasses were becoming more and more influential. With IMIN’s passion to make the audience’s experience always better than it was before, this mindset is great for always having the consumer’s likes and dislikes in our minds.
