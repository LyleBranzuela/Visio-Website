Technology

With the rise of technology, the eyes are more strained than the previous generations, because of this, glasses are on demand in the market, this is the main idea of why Visio wanted to tackle the eyeglass market, as more and more complaints are being received in everyday life, making Visio, a company on answering these complaints.

Glass

The eyeglasses’ lenses are made from a wide variety of materials ranging from Polycarbonate to Crown Glass, each has a variety of Refractive Index and Abbe Value. The lenses can also be layered with colour to become a sunglasses.

Frame

The frames of our eyeglasses are made of a titanium-based alloy also called “Memory Metal”, Titanium is a silver-gray metal that’s lightweight, durable, strong, and corrosion resistant.

Design and Colour

Wide range of colours to choose from, as well as multiple design that the customer may customize their own.

Multiple Shapes

Eyeglasses all come from multiple shapes, circles, squares, oblongs, rectangles.
